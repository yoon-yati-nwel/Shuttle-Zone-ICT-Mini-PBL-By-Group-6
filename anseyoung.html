An Seyoung 

            An Se-young is a South Korean badminton player from Gwangju, who won the gold medal at the 2024 Summer Olympics in the women's
            singles event.She was named 2019’s Most Promising Player of the Year and 2023's Female Player of the Year by the BWF.
            23 years (February 5, 2002)
            Height: 1.7 m
            Coach: Rony Agustinus
            Career record: 401 wins, 74 losses
