About This Website

Shuttle Zone is a student-developed badminton information website created to help players, beginners, and sports lovers learn more about the game. From the history of badminton to its rules, equipment, and major tournaments, this website provides clear and easy-to-understand information.

The website includes:

  • Badminton history and origins
  • Official rules and scoring system
  • Equipment guide with images
  • Championship categories and matches
  • Interactive dropdown menus and accordion content

Designed with simplicity and a clean layout, Shuttle Zone aims to make learning about badminton fun and engaging. All content and design were created by Group 6 for an educational project.
