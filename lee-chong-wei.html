Lee Chong Wei 

        Lee Chong Wei is a Malaysian former professional badminton player. As a singles player, Lee was ranked first worldwide for 349 weeks, including a 
        199-week streak from 21 August 2008 to 14 June 2012.
        Born: October 21, 1982 (age 43 years), Bagan Serai, Malaysia
        Spouse: Wong Mew Choo (m. 2012)
        Medals: Badminton at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Men's singles 
        Children: Anson Lee
        Siblings: Lee Chong Hoon
        height: 1.72 m
        Parents: Lee Ah Chai, Khor Kim Choi
