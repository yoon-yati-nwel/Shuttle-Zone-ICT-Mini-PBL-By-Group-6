Wang Zhiyi 

            Wang Zhiyi is a Chinese badminton player from Shashi, Jingzhou, in Hubei province. She was two-time Asian
            Champions winning the title in 2022 and 2024. She was part of China winning team in the 2020
            and 2024 Uber Cup, as well at the 2021 and 2023 Sudirman Cup. She won her first BWF World Tour Finals title in 2024.
            Born: April 29, 2000 (age 25 years), Shashi District, Jingzhou, China
            Medals: Badminton at the 2022 Asian Games – Women's teams · See more
            Teammates: Chen Yufei, Han Yue, Huang Dongping, Jia Yifan · See more
            Height: 1.71 m
            Weight: 65 kg
            Coach: Luo Yigang
            Current ranking: 2 (21 October 2025)
